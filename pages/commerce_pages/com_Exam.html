A Chartered Accountant (CA) is a professional accountant who has completed a rigorous training program and passed a series of examinations in financial management, auditing, business strategy, and taxation

Company Secretary Executive Entrance Exam (CSEET)

The CSSET (Combined Shiksha Shastri Entrance Test) is a national-level entrance examination conducted by Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri Rashtriya Sanskrit Vidyapeeth, New Delhi. The exam aims to provide admission to B.Ed programs in Sanskrit at three Sanskrit deemed universities across India.



ACET

The Actuarial Common Entrance Test (ACET) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the Institute of Actuaries of India (IAI) to screen students for admission to actuarial science courses. It is a mandatory exam for individuals who do not fall into the 18 categories of reserved seats for actuarial education and training.



SSC

Purpose: To recruit personnel for Group B and C posts in various ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India.
Exams: SSC conducts various national-level exams, including: SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level)

DUJAT

Conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS), Bachelor of Business Administration (Financial Investment Analysis), and BA (Hons) in Business Economics offered by Delhi University.



NMIMS NPAT

Conducted by Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) for undergraduate programs like BBA, B.Com, BSc, BTech, and BA Hons.



CUET

Conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA) for undergraduate admissions at 44 central universities and 46 other institutions.
